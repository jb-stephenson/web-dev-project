Cove Lily Motel

While left vacant during the encampment of Team Magma and Team Aqua, the Cove Lily Motel and the rest of Lily Cove City were saved by an unknown trainer. The Cove Lily Motel has two floors. On the first floor, one can find the kitchen to the left, some chairs and tables for a common area, a television, and sometimes the motel's friendly neighborhood Kecleon. There is also a vending machine on the first floor. On the second floor, one can find the guest rooms. We have several rooms available, while one room is always reserved by the graphic artist, the programmer, and the game designer of the GameFreak team in the Hoenn region. Some of the nearby landmarks include the Lily Cove Museum, the Hoenn Safari Zone, Mt. Pyre, and the recently deserted hideout of Team Magma and Team Aqua.
